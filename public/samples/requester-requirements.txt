SYNTHETIC SAMPLE - REQUESTER INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

Certificate holder name: Northbridge Development LLC
Certificate holder address: 100 Main Street, Rochester, NY 14604
Special wording: None

1. Commercial General Liability Insurance
The contractor shall provide and maintain Commercial General Liability insurance with limits of not less than $1,000,000 each occurrence and $2,000,000 general aggregate. Coverage shall apply on an occurrence basis.

2. Additional Insured
Northbridge Development LLC shall be included as an additional insured on the Commercial General Liability policy by endorsement.

3. Waiver of Subrogation
The contractor shall provide a waiver of subrogation in favor of Northbridge Development LLC where permitted by law.

4. Umbrella or Excess Liability
The contractor shall provide Umbrella or Excess Liability coverage with a limit of not less than $5,000,000.
